## Why

BYOM rollout requires operator visibility into provider health and fallback behavior to be supportable in production. Without diagnostics and minimal controls, incident resolution and policy governance will be slow and error-prone.

## What Changes

- Define operator diagnostics surface for provider and pipeline health.
- Define minimum control set for safe operations.
- Define required telemetry for fallback and verification outcomes.

## Capabilities

### New Capabilities
- `operator-diagnostics-and-controls`: Operational diagnostics and control requirements for BYOM runtime and policy pipeline.

### Modified Capabilities
- None.

## Impact

- Improves supportability, observability, and recovery operations.
- Provides operational guardrails without enabling managed-mode UX.
