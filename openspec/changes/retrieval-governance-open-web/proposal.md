## Why

Open-web retrieval is required for up-to-date responses, but it needs explicit trust ranking and rejection policies to avoid low-quality or stale evidence entering verified outputs.

## What Changes

- Define open-web retrieval governance policy.
- Define source trust scoring and minimum quality thresholds.
- Define freshness windows and stale-evidence handling.
- Define contradiction handling for conflicting sources.

## Capabilities

### New Capabilities
- `retrieval-governance-open-web`: Source quality, trust ranking, freshness, and contradiction governance for open-web retrieval.

### Modified Capabilities
- None.

## Impact

- Affects evidence retrieval quality and verification outcomes.
- Improves correctness posture for time-sensitive recommendations.
